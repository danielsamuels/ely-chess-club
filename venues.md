For players playing away matches, here are the address details for each club as well as any notes on parking arrangements.

| Team              | Address                                                                                     | Parking Notes                                                                                                                                                                                    |
|-------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Ely               | Beet Sports & Social Club, 83 Lynn Rd, Ely CB6 1DE                                          | Access from Lynn Road, with a large car park in front of the building.                                                                                                                           |
| Linton            | The Cathodeon Centre, High St, Linton CB21 4JT                                              | If you are early, you may find the car park locked. Someone should come along and open it. There's space for around 12 cars in front of the building.                                            |
| Newmarket         | Exning Road Working Mens Club, 54 Exning Road, Newmarket, CB8 0AB                           | Parking is available on the opposite side of the road, just past the pedestrian island outside the building.                                                                                     |
| Sudbury           | The Quay Theatre, Quay Ln, Sudbury CO10 2AN                                                 | There is parking directly in front of the building, but you may need to enter your registration number into an iPad at reception.                                                                |
| St Ives / Warboys | The St Ives Ivy Leaf Club Ltd, 23 The Broadway, St Ives, St. Ives PE27 5BX, UK              | Parking is available on the street, entrance is through the archway then the door is on your left.                                                                                               |
| New England       | 	City of Peterborough Sports Club, Dalrod Sports Ground, Bretton Gate, Peterborough PE3 9UZ | Huge car park on site, you need to enter your registration in the main entrance area.
