For players playing away matches, here are the address details for each club as well as any notes on parking arrangements.

## Bury St Edmunds
[Moreton Hall Community Centre, Symonds Rd, Bury Saint Edmunds IP32 7EE](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJHxgB9_BM2EcR3HEQPmn3ZIM&query=Moreton+Hall+Community+Centre,+Symonds+Rd,+Bury+Saint+Edmunds+IP32+7EE)

Large car park in front, no need to register your number.

## Ely
[Ely Beet Sports & Social Club, 83 Lynn Rd, Ely CB6 1DE](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJZSti55UW2EcRdRQiLMIbzKk&query=Ely+Beet+Sports+%26+Social+Club,+83+Lynn+Rd,+Ely+CB6+1DE)

Access from Lynn Road, large car park in front of the building.

## Godmanchester
[Comrades Club, 58 Cambridge Street, Godmanchester, Huntingdon Cambs PE29 2AY](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJK58XmyDdd0gRg2lQe7fPxPM&query=Comrades+Club,+58+Cambridge+Street,+Godmanchester,+Huntingdon+Cambs+PE29+2AY)

Approach from St Ann's Lane; medium-sized car park with visible entrance.

## Linton
[The Cathodeon Centre, High St, Linton CB21 4JT](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJc1CMBftj2EcRipC-kfffICw)

If you are early the car park may be locked; someone should open it. Space for around 12 cars in front of the building.

## New England
[City of Peterborough Sports Club, Dalrod Sports Ground, Bretton Gate, Peterborough PE3 9UZ](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJE4_Jq6fxd0gRbR04Rkfj544&query=City+of+Peterborough+Sports+Club,+Dalrod+Sports+Ground,+Bretton+Gate,+Peterborough+PE3+9UZ)

Huge on-site car park; enter your registration at the main entrance. The playing room is at the back, accessible through a door you pass on the way in.

## Newmarket
[Exning Road Working Mens Club, 54 Exning Road, Newmarket CB8 0AB](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJrxMPc3JC2EcRgN5KvKFXr2U&query=Exning+Road+Working+Mens+Club,+54+Exning+Road,+Newmarket,+CB8+0AB)

Available on the opposite side of the road, just past the pedestrian island outside the building.

## Peterborough
[Angle Gaming Lounge, 939 Lincoln Rd, Walton, Peterborough PE4 6AF](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJTfg9kgLxd0gRGIZIgrXtUJs&query=Angle+Gaming+Lounge,+939+Lincoln+Rd,+Walton,+Peterborough+PE4+6AF)

## St Ives / Warboys
[The St Ives Ivy Leaf Club Ltd, 23 The Broadway, St Ives PE27 5BX](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJPySn2mbed0gRbPYG_P5oJWI&query=The+St+Ives+Ivy+Leaf+Club+Ltd,+23+The+Broadway,+St+Ives,+St.+Ives+PE27+5BX,+UK)

On-street parking; entrance through the archway then the door is on your left.

## St Neots
[St Neots Conservative Club, 12 New St, Saint Neots PE19 1AE](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJCSqVz8vFd0gR6rHZL359sCE&query=St+Neots+Conservative+Club,+12+New+St,+Saint+Neots+PE19+1AE)

No on-site parking; use Tebutts Road Long Stay around the corner (free after 6PM).

## Stowmarket
[Rookery Bowling Club, Finborough Road, Stowmarket IP14 1PN](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJhfHcOa262UcR6T5ESEIv9KU&query=Rookery+Bowling+Club,+Finborough+Road,+Stowmarket,+IP14+1PN)

Entrance is easy to miss; once in, go to the bottom section where it widens out. The entrance is a small porch; it can be cold, so wear layers.

## Sudbury
[The Quay Theatre, Quay Ln, Sudbury CO10 2AN](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query_place_id=ChIJJ-8PpnZV2EcR9tAGMV_4cPQ&query=The+Quay+Theatre,+Quay+Ln,+Sudbury+CO10+2AN)

Parking directly in front; you may need to enter your registration number into an iPad at reception.
